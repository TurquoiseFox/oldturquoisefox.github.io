It's the 8th of September of the year 1970. I was conceived.
My birth on the 8th of June 1971.
1976: I start singing. Loved to sing to something Dutch called "Kinderen voor Kinderen". Later on, I start singing to music of Fame, Michael Jackson, Grease and Stevie Wonder.
1980: Soundmix-shows become totally hip and I start singing in these type of competitions.
In 1992 I discover the world of bands. Love to make music with other musicians!
I become a mother and singing lullabies for my child is the only singing I'll do for some years.
2000: The need to sing starts again. My friend Linn and I start a new vocal group: Soulmates. For this music adventure I start maintaining the content for our website, using Dreamweaver. My interest for building web applications has been roared. Though it won't be something I will seriously pursue for more than a decade.
2005: Soulmates ends and I start my own band: Sylvia's Groove! that will excist for 2 years. These are the cover photo's for the band website.
It's 2008. My mariage ends, I start singing in a party band called: Superfly, where I'll sing for the next 5 years. For this band I'm in charge of the vocal arrangements, next to being one of the lead singers. Also, during this period I start my own school for singers, have an other baby and remarry.
Fall of 2013: I start learning skills for coding just for fun on online courses. I re-discover how much I enjoy doing this, so I decide to see if anyone can offer me a place where I can learn to be a professional at it. A local company near my house offers me an internship and here I learn and work for the next year and a half. In the summer of 2014 I quit my school for singers and focus completely on my new career as a software developer. Also, I start singing my own songs on stage.
Fall of 2015: I start working for Sogeti Nederland. A fun employer who really knows how to put it's employees in their strength. Proud to work here! 2016: my first job for Raet (a client of Sogeti) in Amersfoort. I had so much fun as Frontend Developer in one of the Scrum teams. 2017: working as Frontend Developer at Centraal Beheer Achmea (a client of Sogeti) in Apeldoorn.
